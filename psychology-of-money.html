No One's Crazy

People do crazy things with money. But no one is actually crazy‚Äîthey're making decisions based on their unique experiences. A person who grew up in poverty views money differently than someone born into wealth. A person who lived through hyperinflation has a different relationship with savings than someone who hasn't.

üîë Key Insight
